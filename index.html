About Us

This workshop offers a holistic exploration of electronics system design, covering a spectrum of essential elements such as hands-on exposure to system design intricacies, circuit design optimization, PCB layout design, and fabrication techniques, equipping the participants with invaluable skills for their electronic design endeavors. With access to specialized test and measurement workbenches, attendees can ensure the accuracy and reliability of their designs. Moreover, they'll engage in rapid prototyping using 3D printing technology, enabling swift system packaging and development. Also, participants will be exposed to the latest trends and advancements in electronic system design that empower them to refine their skills and acquire practical knowledge essential for success in the field.

Curriculum Elevation: Crafting the Course Modules

Trending Frontiers In IoT and Industrial IoT

Explore the fundamentals of IoT through real-world use cases with microcontrollers. Gain hands-on experience in real-time sensor data logging and develop interactive mobile applications/web interface for smart device control.

Robotics And Drone Technology

Robotics and Drone Technology introduces you to the world of intelligent machines and autonomous systems. Learn the core principles of robotic control, sensor integration, and the mechanics of drones, along with their applications in automation, surveillance, and remote operations.

Microcontroller Units

Think of Microcontroller Units (MCUs) as tiny, programmable brains for electronics. This module teaches you how to make these brains control things like lights, motors, and sensors, which is key for building smart gadgets and IoT devices.

Elecronics Design And Fabrication

Electronics Design and Fabrication covers the complete journey from circuit concept to realization, emphasizing hands-on skills in prototyping and assembly. Learn techniques for optimizing electronic systems through practical design and fabrication processes, including printed circuit development.

Assembly And Troubleshooting Using Test And Measurement Instruments

Assembly and Troubleshooting Using Test and Measurement Instruments focuses on developing practical skills to inspect electronic systems. Learn to effectively use diagnostic tools for analyzing circuit behavior, identifying faults, and ensuring optimal functionality and reliability in electronic assemblies.

Rapid Prototyping With 3D Printing

Awareness with the Rapid Prototyping using 3D Printing; Learn to design, test, and iterate prototypes efficiently, enabling faster product development and innovation in electronics and mechanical systems.
